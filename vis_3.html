Public Transport Journeys in London

Context & Question. This visualization examines how the number of different public transport journeys in London changes over time. The goal is to understand long-term usage trends and how transport compares across years.

Design. A bar chart is used to show yearly journey volumes clearly, while a dashed line highlights the year-to-year trend. Bar height represents journeys, and color encodes total public transport journeys to provide broader context. This approach balances clarity and context.

Key Insights. Despite differences across transport modes, the sharpest decline in journeys occurs during the COVID-19 period. Cable cars are consistently the least used mode of public transport, while buses account for the highest number of journeys. This highlights both the unequal popularity of transport modes and the strong impact of external shocks on mobility demand.

Data source: github - london-transport
