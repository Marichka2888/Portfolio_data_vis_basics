Chart 4: London Air Pollution

Context & Question. This visualization explores how different gasses affect air pollution levels and how they vary across London during 2008-2018 years. Understanding pollution patterns helps identify periods with higher health risks and analyse reasons.

Design. An interactive Tableau visualization is used to allow filtering by years and gass type. Color and values show deviation from longterm average in absolute value, enabling quick comparison across time. Bar chart is the most effective for such comparison.

Key Insights. SO2 pollution is the most unstable in its changes. NO2 pollution is consistently well above average. They need additional focus.

Data source: github - london-air-quality
